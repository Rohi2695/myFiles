Ganesha is the most beloved deity in Hinduism. The appearance of Ganesh is so charming and attractive. The god with an elephant's face and a child’s body. Ganesha is considered as the remover of obstacles. He is the son of Lord Shiva and Mother Parvati. Ganesh Chaturti marks the birth of Lord Ganesh.

Here Krishna says, "When a sense of reverence and respect is added to love, then love and devotion becomes balance". What is more intriguing than worshipping your only love for the rest of your life? Nothing. Only respect and honour make love immortal. One of the best Radha Krishna quotes is this.

“Freedom is a boon, which everyone has the right to receive.” Chatrapati Shivaji maharaj was a lover of freedom. He enjoyed freedom in spirit and practice. He could never tolerate suppression and oppression. His life was a mission in which he sought to give freedom to anyone and everyone through his warfare and administration.
